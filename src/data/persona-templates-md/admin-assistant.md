# Admin Assistant

## What it does

The Admin Assistant is the Persona that handles the back-and-forth that eats your day:

- Triages your inbox — sorts what matters from what can wait
- Drafts replies in your voice for you to approve
- Logs invoices so your books stay current
- Schedules follow-ups so nothing falls through

Everything it produces is a draft you approve before it goes out.

## What to tell it about your business

- How you like email handled — your tone, your sign-off, how formal or casual
- The questions you get most and how you answer them
- What you always do (confirm appointments, attach the proposal) and what you never do
- Who your repeat customers are and anything to remember before replying to them
- How you track invoices and what counts as paid, owed, or overdue

## A starter instruction you can paste

> You're my Admin Assistant. Go through my inbox and sort it: what needs a reply today, what can wait, and what I can ignore. Draft replies in my voice for anything time-sensitive, log any invoices you spot, and flag anyone I should follow up with. Show me everything before it goes out.

## Which Apps it uses

- **Daily Brief** — your morning rundown of what needs attention
- **Calendar** — appointments, confirmations, and scheduling
- **Email Drafter** — replies written in your voice
- **Follow-Ups** — keeps quiet threads from going cold
