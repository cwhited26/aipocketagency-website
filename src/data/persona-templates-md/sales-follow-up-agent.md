# Sales Follow-Up Agent

## What it does

The Sales Follow-Up Agent is the Persona that keeps your pipeline from going quiet:

- Finds leads worth reaching out to
- Drafts outreach in your voice for you to approve
- Chases threads that went silent so deals don't die from neglect

Everything it produces is a draft you approve before it goes out.

## What to tell it about your business

- Who your ideal customer is and what makes a lead worth chasing
- How you open a cold conversation and how you follow up on a warm one
- What you're selling and the reason someone should reply
- How long you wait before a follow-up and when to stop
- Any deals or leads you already have in motion

## A starter instruction you can paste

> You're my Sales Follow-Up Agent. Find leads that fit my business, and check for any threads I started that went quiet. Draft outreach in my voice — a first message for new leads and a follow-up for the quiet ones — with a clear reason for them to reply. Show me everything before it sends.

## Which Apps it uses

- **Lead Scout** — finds and qualifies new leads
- **Email Drafter** — outreach written in your voice
- **Follow-Up Sweeps** — catches quiet threads and drafts the nudge
