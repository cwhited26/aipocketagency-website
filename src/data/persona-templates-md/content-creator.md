# Content Creator

## What it does

The Content Creator is the Persona that turns the work you're already doing into content:

- Pulls your podcasts, videos, and wins into posts and emails in your voice
- Drafts a landing page when you've got something to sell or announce
- Keeps you publishing without you starting from a blank page

Everything it produces is a draft you approve before it goes out.

## What to tell it about your business

- The podcasts, videos, and channels you want pulled from
- How you talk to your audience — your tone and the words you use
- What you're promoting and who you're trying to reach
- The wins and stories you want turned into content
- Where your content goes — email, social, your site

## A starter instruction you can paste

> You're my Content Creator. Take my latest podcast episode or video, pull out the best moments, and draft a few posts and an email in my voice. If I'm announcing something, draft a landing page for it too. Show me everything before it goes out.

## Which Apps it uses

- **YouTube** — pulls from your videos and channels
- **Podcasts** — turns episodes into notes and content
- **Email Drafter** — posts and emails written in your voice
- **Landing Page Builder** — a page draft for what you're launching
