# Business Brain Setup Checklist

Your agents only know what you tell them. The Business Brain is where you put everything about how your business actually runs — your offers, your voice, your customers — so the work that comes back sounds like you wrote it. Fill it once and every Persona pulls from it.

- [ ] **Your offers.** Add what you sell and what it costs. This is what the agents quote, pitch, and point people toward, so it has to match what you'd say on a call.
- [ ] **Your services.** List each service with a plain description of what's included. The agents use this to answer "what do you do" and to scope work in drafts.
- [ ] **Your FAQs.** Drop in the questions you answer over and over — hours, pricing, turnaround, what you don't do. Now the agents handle them in your words instead of guessing.
- [ ] **Your brand voice.** Paste a few real emails or posts you've written. This is how the agents learn to sound like you and not like a template.
- [ ] **Your customer notes.** Add who you work with, repeat clients, and anything you'd want remembered before a follow-up. The agents use it to make outreach feel personal.
- [ ] **Your sales materials.** Upload proposals, one-pagers, and pitch decks. The agents borrow your real language and proof points instead of inventing them.
- [ ] **Your past prompts and instructions.** Save the directions you find yourself repeating — how you like emails signed off, what to never say, your standard process. The agents stop needing to be told twice.

The more of this you give them, the sharper every Persona gets.
