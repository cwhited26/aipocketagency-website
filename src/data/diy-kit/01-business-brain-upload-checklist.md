# Business Brain Upload Checklist

Your AI office only knows what you tell it. The Business Brain is where all of that lives — your offers, your voice, your customers — and it's what every agent pulls from before it drafts a thing. Gather and organize these seven items now so that on day one you drop them in instead of hunting for them.

- [ ] **Your offers.** Write down what you sell and what it costs. This is what the agents will quote and pitch, so it has to match what you'd say on a call.
- [ ] **Your services.** List each service with a plain description of what's included. The agents use this to answer "what do you do" and to scope work.
- [ ] **Your FAQs.** Collect the questions you answer over and over — hours, pricing, turnaround, what you don't do — with your real answers next to them.
- [ ] **Your voice samples.** Pull a few real emails or posts you've written. This is how the agents learn to sound like you instead of a template.
- [ ] **Your customer list.** Put together who you work with, repeat clients, and notes you'd want remembered before a follow-up.
- [ ] **Your sales materials.** Round up your proposals, one-pagers, and pitch decks so the agents borrow your real language and proof points.
- [ ] **Your standard instructions.** Note the directions you repeat — how you sign off, what to never say, your standard process — so you only have to say them once.

Have these ready in a folder and your first day in the product is mostly just uploading.
