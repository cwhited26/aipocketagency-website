# AI Office DIY Setup Kit

This is the set-it-up-yourself version of getting an AI office running for your business. It's the same checklists, the same 7-day plan, and the same brain-loading guide the team uses for done-with-you setup — packaged so you can do the work on your own time before you ever pay for help.

It's for the owner who'd rather do it themselves. If you like understanding how a thing works before you buy it, if you'd rather spend a weekend setting it up than hand it off, this is for you. You'll come out the other side knowing exactly what your AI office knows and how to run it.

Here's how to use it. Work the 7-day plan one day at a time. Use the checklists to gather and organize your business — your offers, your voice, your customers — so it's ready to drop in. When you sign up at aipocketagent.com, import the JSON bundle and everything you prepared loads in at once.

Everything in this kit is also free inside the product once you subscribe. This kit exists for one reason: so you can show up on day one already set up, instead of starting from a blank page.
